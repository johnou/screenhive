What is Screen Hive?

Have you always wanted to watch videos and movies with your friends over the internet without using annoying methods like, telling everyone to hit play at the same time? Then Screen Hive is the service for you!

Screen Hive is a system of virtual browsers running in the cloud, that provide near to realtime streaming of videos, movies, anime and general internet browsing to many users in a room. Each room can have up to 20 people watching at a time!
